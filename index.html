About

Shalom Blue is a Nigerian/Canadian singer-songwriter from Ajax, Ontario. He specializes ambient influenced altRnb. His music has been described as similar to the styles of Frank Ocean and Daniel Caesar. As a selected finalist of the 2024 Rise and Unity Charity LCA program, he received the opportunity to be an artist opener for JUNO award-winning musician ToBi. Since then he has continued his journey to captivate audiences through ethereal melodies.
