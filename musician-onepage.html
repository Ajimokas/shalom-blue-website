About

Shalom Blue is a Nigerian/Canadian singer-songwriter from Ajax, Ontario. He specializes in a unique take of the RnB genre that blends the influences of ambient music, pop and Neo-soul, which reflect his diverse musical background. His latest single, “Delusional” is an upbeat afrobeat tune celebrating the feeling of overwhelming love. His music has been described as similar to the styles of Frank Ocean and Daniel Caesar. As a selected finalist of the 2024 Rise and Unity Charity LCA program, he received the opportunity to be an artist opener for JUNO award-winning musician ToBi. Since then he has continued his journey to captivate audiences through ethereal melodies.
